Bio

I am a postgraduate student at the University of Oxford studying for an MPhil in Politics (Comparative Government). I also have a BA from Oxford, having studied Philosophy, Politics and Economics as an undergraduate.

Outside of University, I am a keen road cyclist. I regularly take part in sportives, and have a particular penchant for hill climbing! My blog is typically about cycling-related matters and routes in/around Oxford or the South Downs.
