Bio

I am a postgraduate student at the University of Oxford studying for an MPhil in Politics (Comparative Government). I also have a BA from Oxford, having studied Philosophy, Politics and Economics as an undergraduate.

Outside of University, I am a keen road cyclist. When not in Oxford, I live in Sussex in between the High Weald and South Downs where there are more than enough hills to keep me distracted. I regularly take part in sportives, and have a particular penchant for hill climbing! My blog is typically cycling-related matters about routes in/around Oxford and the South Downs.
