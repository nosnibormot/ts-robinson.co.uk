Bio

I am a postgraduate student at the University of Oxford studying for an MPhil in Politics (Comparative Government). I also studied at Oxford as an undergraduate, and read for a degree in Philosophy, Politics and Economics.

During my time at university as an undergrad, I worked on the student newspaper Cherwell as Comment Editor and subsequently on the senior editorial team (Trinity 2015).

Outside of University, I am a keen road cyclist. I regularly take part in sportives, and have a particular passion for hill climbing! My blog tends to be about cycling and routes in the Oxfordshire/Buckinghamshire area and the South Downs.
